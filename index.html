How HarzAjo Works

1. Create or Join a Group
Set contribution amount, frequency (daily/weekly/monthly), and member count.

2. Contribute Every Cycle
Pay your contribution via Paystack. Automatic tracking and reminders.

3. Receive Your Payout
Each cycle, one member receives the total pot. Rotation continues until everyone gets paid.

4. Build Trust
BVN/NIN verification (via Smile ID) ensures all members are real. Admin manages payout order.

Example:
5 members × ₦50,000 weekly = ₦250,000 pot. Each week one person collects ₦250,000. After 5 weeks, everyone has received their payout.
